Bacon and Eggs

Click Here

Bacon, eggs, and toast, it doesn't get any better in my opinion. Every Sunday I look forward to this staple. Thick cut smoked bacon, farm fresh eggs, and fresh bread, it doesn't get any better than that.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2-3 strips of bacon
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1.5 tablespoons margarine or butter
  • salt
  • pepper

Directions

  1. melt 0.5 tablespoons margarine or butter in frying pan
  2. place bacon in frying pan and cook at medium heat
  3. place bread in toaster
  4. when bacon is half cooked, start bread toasting
  5. place eggs in frying pan
  6. when toast done, butter toast with remainig margarine or butter
  7. remove bacon from pan and dab with paper towel to remove excess grease
  8. cook eggs to preferred style
  9. place eggs, bacon, and toast on plate and enjoy...
